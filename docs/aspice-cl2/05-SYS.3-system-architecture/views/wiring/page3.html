Board-to-Board Connections

Non-ECU connections: isoSPI, contactors, cell emulator.

1:1 physical wiring between boards. No MCU involved — these are board-to-board or board-to-sensor connections.

Interface Board ↔ Slave Board (isoSPI Daisy Chain)

Transformer-coupled galvanic isolation | 1 Mbps | PEC-15 HD=6 | Max 100m (practical <2m) | 2s watchdog

SPS IC ↔ Contactors (Physical Actuation)

SPS output drives contactor coil. Spring-return mechanism = fail-safe open.

Slave Cell Connector ↔ Battery Cells / Emulator

24-pin connector | Interleaved pinout | 19 channels for emulator
